Internship Opportunities

At our company we are looking to find the graphic designers of tomorrow. We find raw, fresh talent of artists and digital creaters to add to our creative team. Not everyone can be a fun and creative individual, but if you fit into this category, we would love for you to apply as an intern. We accept interns as young as eighteen years old, and after the internship is over and you decide you do not want to leave our company, we will consider adding you to our team as a part time or full time employee.

As an intern we teach the ways of our buisness, and set new designers up for a successful future in graphic design. You will be taught the best ways to create digital media on the latest Adobe softwares. You will also learn the best strategies on how to market and sell to a buisness. We also offer to teach interns the basics on web design, to see if they strive more in coding. Through an internship with us there is so much opportunity to learn, grow, and all around become a better designer.

Contact Information

If you are a company wanting to work with us, are wanting to get in contact, or just have any questions you may want to ask, feel free to get in touch with our company. We have staff who are prepared to help you in whatever ways that they can.

If you are looking to become an intern please fill out our form to apply. We will try our best to look through applications and get back to you within a week. If we have not replied within a week, feel free to get in contact with our staff so asure we did not miss you.
